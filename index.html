About Us

Behind the Lens

We don't just take photographs — we preserve the poetry of human experience.

Founded in 2018, Loyal Studio is a boutique photography and videography collective based in New York City. Our team of passionate visual storytellers specializes in capturing authentic moments that resonate long after the shutter clicks.

From intimate portraits to grand landscapes, from whispered wedding vows to bold commercial campaigns, we approach every project with the same dedication: to create images that feel alive, honest, and deeply personal.
